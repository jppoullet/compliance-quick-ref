Downgrading accounts due to missing equipment or services
If disconnecting an account because it is missing equipment Account is part of acp program. This delinquent account has been disconnected due to not having equipment. Customer must pay delinquent balance to add video, voice or internet. If customer calls to restore services please set them up with ACP internet offer 29.99 and ship modem/router.
If disconnecting due to NO HSD code + no equip Account is part of acp program. This delinquent account has been disconnected due to not having internet equipment. Customer must pay delinquent balance to add video, voice or internet. If customer calls to restore services please set them up with ACP internet offer 29.99 and ship modem/router.
NO HSD but has active HSD equipment EBB/ACP DE-ENROLLED: NO INTERNET USAGE DETECTED- ACCOUNT DISCONNECTED. TO RE-ENROLL LOCATE ORIGINAL UCM TICKET, RE-OPEN AND RE-SUBMIT. REFER TO HOW6832 FOR DETAILS ON RE-OPENING EBB/ACP UCM TICKETS
If taking Wifi pods off Customer must pay balance in full before adding wifi pods back to account

Escalation buckets and reasons

Bucket Name Reason to Escalate
Billing TMS This bucket is for accounts that have had Billing TMS tickets submitted due to stuck orders. Include the ticket number in your escalation.
PAC UCM

This bucket is for accounts that have had PAC UCM tickets submitted due to stuck Change of Service or Install orders. Include the ticket number in your escalation.


Use the following standardized note in your UCM ticket:

Need sodi cleared so work order completes so non pay can be entered

Commercial This bucket is for accounts that are commerial accounts
Future Bill This bucket is for accounts that have orders that are future billed that cannot be canceled or completed until their bill date. This is to be used if the date is over 30 days out, or if the account has already been future billed in TARRMS at least once before
InHouse This bucket is for Spectrum In-House accounts. - Do not credit, cancel NPDs or otherwise modify the account before escalating. This bucket is NOT for employee accounts. Employee accounts should be worked BAU.
ISP05E SERVICES NEED MORE EQ This bucket is for accounts that have "needs more equipment" issues that are unable to be remedied with placeholders or outlet corrections.
New Issue Pending Research This bucket is only to be escalated to when directed to by a member of leadership. This bucket is for issues in need of further research. Include the name of the leader who directed you to escalate to this bucket.
