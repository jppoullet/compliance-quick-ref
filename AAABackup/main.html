Escalation buckets and reasons

Bucket Name Reason to Escalate
Billing TMS This bucket is for accounts that have had Billing TMS tickets submitted due to stuck orders. Include the ticket number in your escalation.
PAC UCM This bucket is for accounts that have had PAC UCM tickets submitted due to stuck COS or Install orders. Include the ticket number in your escalation.
Commercial This bucket is for accounts that are commerial accounts
Future Bill This bucket is for accounts that have orders that are future billed that cannot be canceled or completed until their bill date. This is to be used if the date is over 30 days out, or if the account has already been future billed in TARRMS at least once before
InHouse This bucket is for Spectrum In-House accounts.
ISP05E SERVICES NEED MORE EQ This bucket is for accounts that have "needs more equipment" issues that are unable to be remedied with placeholders or outlet corrections.
New Issue Pending Research This bucket is only to be escalated to when directed to by a member of leadership.
